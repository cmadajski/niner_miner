What is Niner Miner?

E-Commerce for the UNCC Community

Niner Miner is a web application similar to services like Facebook Marketplace or OfferUp where people can buy or sell items to one another in an open marketplace.

What separates Niner Miner is that all accounts are linked to a UNCC email address, which decreases bad behavior and sketchy deals.

Anyone who violates the Terms of Service can and will be banned based on the severity of their infraction and that email will be blacklisted from the Niner Miner service indefinitely.

Likewise, items can only be sold on campus, so there's no need to worry about shipping. You can select one of many locations on campus to meet with a buyer or seller before committing to a purchase.

Requirements

In order to use the Niner Miner application, you will need:

  • an active UNCC email address
  • your UNCC ID number

After sign up, a verification code will be sent to the given email to help verify that you are actually a UNCC student and not some imposter!
